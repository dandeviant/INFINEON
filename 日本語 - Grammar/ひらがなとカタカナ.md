Hiragana and Katakana

( ー ) extend the vowels of the syllables before

> Example けー　　= ke-e ケーキ　＝ ke-e-ki　(cake)

( ～ ) wave dash

> indicate range or intervals (example: ５～6 分(ぶ bu）/ 5 or 6 minutes
